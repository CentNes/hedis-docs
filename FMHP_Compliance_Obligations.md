# First Medical Health Plan — HEDIS/Quality Compliance Obligations (1-page)

_The binding must-dos for a Plan Vital (PR Medicaid) MCO, distilled from
`PR_HEDIS_Requirements_Library.md`. Authorities in brackets. **Verify figures/deadlines against the
executed ASES Model Contract and licensed NCQA materials — not legal advice.**_

## What FMHP must report
| Obligation | Cadence | Authority |
|---|---|---|
| **HEDIS measure set** selected by ASES each calendar year | Annual | ASES Model Contract; 42 CFR 438.340(b)(3)(i) |
| **CMS Adult + Child Core Sets** (Child Core Set mandatory; BH Adult measures mandatory) + **EPSDT** | Annual | ASES Model Contract; Consolidated Appropriations Act / CMS |
| **CAHPS** — Adult, Child, and ECHO (behavioral health) surveys | ≥ Annual | ASES Model Contract |
| **HCIP quality measures** (Healthy People, Chronic Conditions, + 3rd initiative) | **Quarterly** | ASES HCIP (Model Contract) |
| Network-access / geographic-access reports | Quarterly | ASES Model Contract; 42 CFR 438.68 |

## Quality-program obligations
- **QAPI program** — annual program description with improvement targets. [42 CFR 438.330]
- **Performance Improvement Projects (PIPs)** — **5 minimum topics**: kidney health (KED), BH/SUD screening, diabetes outcomes, EPSDT, and PH/BH integration; submitted annually, **validated by the EQRO**. [42 CFR 438.330(d), 438.358]
- **External Quality Review** — cooperate with the ASES EQRO (**Mercer**); results published in the annual EQR Technical Report. [42 CFR 438.350–364]

## Financial exposure
- **HCIP quality withhold** — a portion of the administrative fee (reported ~**2%**; *confirm in the Model Contract*) is **withheld and earned back quarterly** by hitting ASES benchmark targets on the HCIP measures. Missing targets = lost dollars. [ASES HCIP]
- **ASES/OCS penalties** for provider non-payment / missing metrics — a live compliance risk area tied to prompt-pay and reporting. [PR Insurance Code Arts. 30.x; OCS]

## Audit & certification (to make results *reportable*)
- **HEDIS Compliance Audit** by a licensed auditor — IS standards (IS 1–7), the **Roadmap**, and **MRRV**. [NCQA HEDIS Compliance Audit]
- Rates must come from **NCQA-certified measure logic** (Measure Certification, per-measure/per-year). [NCQA]
- **Submit** via NCQA **IDSS** + **Patient-Level Data**; supplemental data must meet standard/non-standard + **PSV** rules (or DAV-validated). [NCQA]

## Operational (PR law)
- **Prompt/clean-claims payment** to providers within statutory timeframes. [Código de Seguros de PR, Arts. 30.010–30.070; enforced by OCS]

## The deadline on the horizon
- NCQA **retires the hybrid method MY2029** and goes **fully digital (FHIR/CQL) MY2030** — administrative/digital data readiness is now a compliance requirement, not an option. [NCQA Future of HEDIS]
